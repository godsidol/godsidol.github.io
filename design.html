Revenge of the Clout EP

The creative direction for OG Lil Jit’s rollout for his first EP. Together we came up with the concept for the cover and the name. It revolved around the idea of everyone in the industry just copying each other, as if they are all clones. As a result the musician made his songs on the EP revolving around the heavily copied style and made it his own by matching powerful subject matter to each song. Hence him being the Jedi in the cover, attempting to fight the empire which is the industry and the clones which are the other artist. So I took this digital piece and made it into a physical piece by getting a DVD case, I measured it to know the dimensions I needed to work with. The art was illustrated by Karl Thomas and applied to the DVD promotion. The success of this project led to the production of a music video for a song off the EP, we also made shirts using the initial illustration in hopes to raise enough money to get one of these videos onto popular music blogs. We ran a facebook, twitter and instagram promo run to reach a wider audience.

Atlas Papercraft Model

I was tasked with coming up with a piece to be on display for DIADOZ first art exhibit. This art exhibit was to be held in Reading, Pennsylvania and was meant to be an interactive exhibit. We went ahead and went with the concept of creating a papercraft statue, and our concept was to revolve around Atlas, to signify the pressures and stress that comes along with creating a new business. We modeled Atlas in Rhino 3D and designed him to be cut out, so that we could assemble him at the art exhibit. A total of 16 hours and 700 pieces later, We were able to have a piece that not only worked for the exhibit but also allowed us to create promotion around it.

Artist Magazine

I will be creating an online artist lookbook magazine, This will serve as an introduction of the artist to the viewer. This will be designed for print and online viewing.

My key audience would be Art lovers and Artist alike, and teenagers ranging from 16 to adults as old as 30 Male. Mainly because these are the demographics on the social medias websites I am planning on.

I was having trouble finding new artist to like, especially with vast amount of artist and mediums to choose from. So I thought, If I’m having this prob lem, I wondered if anybody else did. I remebered picking up the XXL magazine just to see the freshman class of new rappers being introduced this year in hopes of finding new music to listen to, So I thought why not do the same but with artist of different mediums.

Goals for this project is successfully design and print the debut issue of the favoured, to showcase artist work, to promote collaberation and unity between artist. To gain support and test if this is something that the internet would want, so that I can continue making issues.

I plan on printing 20 copies for each of the artist included in the magazine and create a video book for online consumption so that anyone can access the magazine.
